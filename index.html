I Orthodoxy (25')

Performers: Priest, Lover 1, Lover 2, Lover 3

Musicians: Keyboard/Organ

1 Introit (10') [inspiration] [inspiration]

A bright, colorful and geometric pattern reminiscent of stained glass is projected onto the back wall of the stage.

After the audience is seated, the organist begins playing “Introit.”

Electronics route the sound to a surround-sound speaker array, exciting the entire hall.

Priest enters from the back of the hall accompanied by Lover 1 and Lover 2 acting as altar-servers. Lover 1 carries a swinging thurible 1 introducing incense into the space.

Performers walk at a stately pace towards the front of the hall and ultimately mount the stage.

2 Breathe (10') [score]

At the conclusion of the organ “Introit,” the Priest commands the audience to rise from their seats with amplified voice.

The priest then leads the audience in “Breathe,” a breathing sequence accompanied with projected text.

3 Homily (5')

Priest tells the audience something he truly believes the audience needs to hear in “Homily.”

Live video collages a close-up of the priest’s face onto the back drop.

Amplified voice becomes increasingly distorted and the video back drop begins to dissolve. Fade to blackout as the Priest’s voice echoes through the hall.

II Asceticism (20')

Musicians: Eight-part Vocal Ensemble

1 Total Abstraction (20') [score] [mar-11.mp3] [inspiration]

Semi-circle of eight chairs is placed on the stage.

Ensemble enters from off-stage and each musician takes a seat.

Live video projects a full-shot of the entire ensemble.

Exeunt as audience’s clapping cross-fades into canned recording of clapping.

III Earthly Delights (35')

Performers: Lover 1, Lover 2, Lover 3

Musicians: Four-part Rock Band

1 22re (10')

Video montage of driving scene projected onto back-drop.

Clapping from Act II cross-fades into simulated car-engine surround-sound. Begin live radio and recorded audio.

Fade to noise as video morphs into a sunset landscape with a car parked in the foreground.

2 Intimacy (15') [score] [mar-10.mp3] [mar-15.mp3]

Enter Lover 1 and Lover 2 carrying a blanket. They set the blanket on the ground before sitting down upon it.

Lovers whisper and embrace. Meaningless small-talk begins to work its into a revealing and difficult disclosure.

Closely placed microphones amplify whispers throughout the hall’s surround sound.

Sunset backdrop transitions to night as the Lovers decide to go to sleep together one last time.
