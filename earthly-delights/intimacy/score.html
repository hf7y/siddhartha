“Intimacy” act III / scene 2 Siddhartha

for 2 performers

duration: 10-15 minutes

tech:

  • 2 lavalier microphones, worn by each performer
  • 1 stereo pair, omni-directional, on stage
  • 1 video projector

set:

  • video projection on backdrop (15 min). the sun setting, the mississippi river in background, a parked car (from III/1) in the foreground.
  • downstage, heavy beige blanket on the ground (prop set during performance)
  • stereo pair pointed upstage

score:

  • enter stage right in silence, one performer with blanket
  • set up the blanket downstage before the microphones
  • (the mood is solemn but not sorrowful. the end is near and you’re both okay with it)
  • break the mood with a joke
  • fall to silence again, but then give a gentle touch showing a comfort without words
  • (beat)
  • make a confession in a whisper, then sit with the silence
  • whisper another, silence. and another, each more desperately true than the last
  • say your last, most honest words
  • lay down to sleep one final time
